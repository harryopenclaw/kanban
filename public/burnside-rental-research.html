Negotiation Notes & What to Ask For

Free rent: 1–3 months free on a 5-year lease is common in the current softening market. Push for this up front.
Tenant Improvement (TI) allowance: Ask for $15–$25/sqft for the office component (~$18k–$37k). Used to build out the office portion to your spec.
TMI cap: Negotiate a cap on annual TMI increases (e.g. max 3–4%/yr). TMI can creep up significantly on multi-year leases.
Gross-up clause: Ensure the lease specifies whether TMI is estimated or actual — and whether reconciliation is annual.
Right of first refusal: If you're taking a smaller unit and may want to expand, ask for ROFR on adjacent space.
