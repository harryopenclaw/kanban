GTM Version 1

Content Development Agent

AI-powered research → ideation → script writing pipeline for Immediac's AI for Business content strategy.

Scoped: Apr 4, 2026 · Status: In Design

Objective

Build an integrated content development agent that monitors trends, generates content ideas, and writes platform-specific scripts — fed by real data from 25 monitored Instagram accounts. Content focus: AI for Business, promoting Immediac's offerings.

📸 Instagram 🎵 TikTok ▶️ YouTube 💼 LinkedIn
Workflow — 5 Stages
1

Research

Pull trend data from monitored accounts and external sources. Identify what's performing, what topics are emerging, and where the gaps are.

  • Daily scrape of 25 Instagram accounts (ExecMap integration)
  • Hashtag + topic trend analysis
  • Competitor content performance signals
  • Industry news + AI business news feeds
Research Layer
2

Content Ideation

Generate a ranked list of content ideas based on research signals, aligned with Alex Hormozi + Daniel Isles frameworks — hooks first, value-dense, repurpose-ready.

  • 10–20 ideas per cycle, ranked by predicted performance
  • Angle variations per platform (Instagram Reel vs LinkedIn post)
  • Hook + core message + CTA for each idea
  • Tie-back to Immediac offerings / AI for business positioning
Ideation Layer
3

Script Writing

Turn approved ideas into full scripts. Platform-specific format, optimized for retention and conversion.

  • Short-form scripts (30–90 sec) for TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts
  • Long-form scripts (5–15 min) for YouTube
  • LinkedIn text posts + carousel outlines
  • Hook, body, CTA structure throughout
Script Layer
4

Review & Approval

Scripts delivered to John for review. Feedback loop to refine tone, messaging, or platform fit.

  • Delivered via iMessage / Discord summary
  • Inline comments / edit requests fed back to agent
  • Version tracking per script
Review Layer
5

Output & Storage

Approved scripts stored in a content library. Ready for recording, scheduling, or repurposing.

  • Content library (structured by platform, topic, date)
  • Repurposing queue — one script → multiple formats
  • Performance tracking post-publish (future phase)
Output Layer
