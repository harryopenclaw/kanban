💰 Hormozi-Style Offers — AI Optimization for Existing Clients

Built on the Hormozi Value Equation: Perceived Value = (Dream Outcome × Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort). Each offer maximizes dream outcome and likelihood while minimizing time and effort for the client.

Offer 1: Free AI Visibility Audit Lead Magnet

Hook: “Your competitors are already showing up in ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity. Are you? We’ll show you in 15 minutes — for free.”

What the client gets:

  • How their business appears (or doesn’t) across AI search: ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Copilot
  • Competitor comparison — who’s winning AI visibility in their space
  • Top 3 quick wins to improve AI discoverability this week
  • Score card: AI Visibility Rating (1–10) across 5 dimensions

Value Equation: Dream Outcome = “know exactly where you stand before competitors pass you” · Likelihood = high (it’s a diagnostic, not a promise) · Time = 15 min · Effort = zero (we do the work)

Delivery: Booked via ManyChat or direct link. Immediac delivers a branded 1-page report + 15-min video walkthrough. Natural transition into Offer 2.

Offer 2: AI-Ready Business Package Core Offer

Hook: “91% of small businesses using AI report higher revenue. We’ll get you there in 30 days — or you don’t pay.”

What the client gets:

  • AI Visibility Optimization — structured data, business profiles, and content tuned for AI discovery
  • AI Content Engine Setup — content calendar + 30 days of AI-optimized posts (using content-creator skill frameworks)
  • AI Customer Workflow — one automated workflow (chatbot, review responses, or email sequences)
  • AI Tool Stack Recommendation — personalized list of 3–5 tools for their industry with setup guides
  • Monthly AI Visibility Report — track progress across AI search surfaces

Value Equation: Dream Outcome = “visible in AI search + automated workflows saving 20+ hrs/month” · Likelihood = high (30-day guarantee) · Time = 30 days · Effort = minimal (done-for-you)

Risk Reversal: 30-day money-back guarantee. If they don’t see measurable improvement in AI visibility score, full refund.

Offer 3: AI for Main Street Act — Grant Application Support Value-Add

Hook: “The federal government is giving small businesses up to $50K in AI tax credits and free training. Most owners don’t even know it exists. We’ll apply for you.”

What the client gets:

  • Eligibility assessment (SBA size standards check)
  • Application prep for SBDC training vouchers ($3,500/employee)
  • Guidance on the 35% tax credit (up to $50K/year)
  • Micro-loan application support (up to $150K)

Value Equation: Dream Outcome = “free money and free training from the government” · Likelihood = high (legislation is live) · Time = we handle it · Effort = zero for them

Positioning: Stack this as a bonus on top of Offer 2 (“We’ll also help you claim your AI tax credits”) or offer standalone to warm leads who aren’t ready for the full package yet.

Offer 4: “AI-Proof Your Business” Workshop Upsell

Hook: “AI adoption is moving faster than electricity, PCs, or the internet. In 90 minutes, we’ll build your AI roadmap so you’re leading — not catching up.”

What the client gets:

  • Live 90-minute workshop (in-person or virtual, up to 5 team members)
  • AI readiness assessment for their specific business
  • Customized 90-day AI implementation roadmap
  • Hands-on tool demos with their actual business data
  • Recording + summary doc they keep

Value Equation: Dream Outcome = “team aligned, roadmap clear, no more guessing” · Likelihood = very high (hands-on, customized) · Time = 90 min · Effort = show up

Stack these to create an irresistible “Grand Slam Offer” — where the value so obviously exceeds the price that saying no feels like losing money:

  2. AI-Ready Business Package (core offer — done-for-you, 30-day guarantee)
  3. Grant Application Support (bonus — “we’ll also get you government money”)
  4. “AI-Proof” Workshop (upsell — team alignment + custom roadmap)

Pitch: “You get AI visibility optimization, 30 days of done-for-you content, an automated workflow, government grant support, AND a team workshop. If after 30 days you don’t see improvement, we refund everything. The only way you lose is by doing nothing.”
